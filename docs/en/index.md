<div class="bt-row">
  <div class="bt-label">About</div>
  <div class="bt-body">
    <p>Belarus Transparency is an independent platform documenting the structures, financial flows, and related public actions of the actors of the Belarusian nominal "opposition" based on open sources.</p>
    <p>All materials are based on verifiable data: state registries, financial reporting, public statements, and journalistic investigations. Our methodology and sources are transparent and open to re-verification. Insider information is used only to formulate minimal hypotheses that can be refuted by the organization under investigation.</p>
  </div>
</div>
